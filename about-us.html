The Kaimab story





OUR HISTORY


At Kaimab, we blend tradition, craftsmanship, and innovation. Although founded in 2012, our story began over 30 years ago,born from a passion for transforming natural materials into timeless fashion accessories.





Craftsmanship and Quality in Every Detail


Every Kaimab piece begins as a vision shaped by hands that honor tradition. From raw materials to refined forms, our artisans combine precision, patience, and passion — ensuring that every curve and texture embodies both heritage and innovation.

It’s not just about creating accessories; it’s about crafting timeless expressions of authenticity and style.





Artisanal Precision and Industrial Innovation


Our production base in Egypt allows us to meet large-scale demands, while our workshop in L’Hospitalet de Llobregat (Barcelona) is dedicated to handcrafted buttons, metal jewelry, and leather accessories. This dual structure unites artisanal precision with industrial innovation.







Expanding into Custom-Made Projects


Since 2025, we have expanded our creative universe to include custom-made projects — from board games made with natural materials to cabinet knobs, perfume caps, and unique home décor items.
