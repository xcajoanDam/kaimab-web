The kaimab story

ONCE UPON A TIME



We started out in a small workshop and gradually our business grew to its current size. Today we combine tradition, innovation and technology to create the perfect detail. Kaimab was officially founded in 2012, bringing to fruition the 40 years of experience in the buttons and accessories industry, since the family has been in this business world since 1972.
